Overview

A LevelUp merchant has one or more locations. Take the example of a chain restaurant with multiple franchises. This restaurant would have a single LUMerchant associated with it, but each store would have its own LULocation.

MapKit

LULocation implements the MKAnnotation protocol. This provides annotation-related information to a map view. The title of the annotation is the result of fullStreetAddress.
